10. One Piece

One Piece, an anime from 1999 based of a boy name Monkey D Luffy, who wants to be the king of pirate and find one piece. On continuing his quest, he meets a skilled swordsman name Roronoa Zoro. Nami who is a greedy thief who has a knack for navigation. Usopp, a great liar who has an affinity for inventing. Sanji, a warrior cook. Chopper, a sentient deer who is also a skilled physician and Robin, a former member of Baroque Works.

Started : 1999-current
Genre : Adventure, Fiction, Fantasy
Imdb ratings - 8.8/10

9. Death Note

This story revolves around a high school genius name Light Yagami who happens to find a mysterious notebook fallen on the ground which belongs to a shinigami namely Ryuk. The notebook can give the user a ability to kill anyone by writing their names in the notebook. So Light takes help of it to punish all criminals and make a new world but detective L picks up the case whereas many criminals are dying abnormally. The rest of the story is about how Yagami and L faced each other.

Started : 2006-2007
Genre : Thriller, Horror, Drama, Suspense, Mystery, Crime
Imdb ratings - 9/10
